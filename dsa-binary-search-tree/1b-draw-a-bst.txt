1b. Draw the BST with the keys - E A S Y Q U E S T I O N

Incorrect:

           [E]
    [A]           [S]
           [Q]          [Y]
    [I]           [U]
           [O][T]
                  [N]

Where:
- [A] and [S] are children of [E]
- [Q] and [Y] are children of [S]
- [U] is a left child of [Y]
- [T] is a left child of [U]
- [I] is a left child of [Q]
- [O] is a right child of [I]
- [N] is a right child of [N]

Correct:

Duplicate of a value is considered greater than the value itself.
